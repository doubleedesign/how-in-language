# How in language?

## Programming syntax and concepts, translated.

::: info About
> I've worked with multiple programming languages to varying extents - whether it be entire websites and apps (or parts of huge apps), a few units at TAFE/uni, personal projects, or teaching. So I am no stranger to doing things like Googling "array_intersect in JavaScript", "Can I do package-private classes in PHP?" (no), and "simple key-value pair collection in C#", and generally translating what things are called and what conventions there are in different languages.
>
> 
>One language I had _not_ worked with to any meaningful extent prior to starting at [Ferocia](https://ferocia.com.au/) was Ruby. And unlike when starting a personal project in C# recently where I went "Wow, the syntax is actually really not that different to my object-oriented PHP", I found myself with much more basic questions like "Ummm so how do I loop through this...collection of some sort?"
> 
> -- <cite>Leesa</cite>
:::

This site is a place to bring together the "translations" learnt when moving between programming languages. I hope that no matter what language you're moving to or from (among those documented here), the guides here will help you use what you already know in other languages to smooth the transition.

The content does lean towards certain languages over others based on author experience, so contributions and corrections are very welcome.
